Our Story

ENJOY AN ECLECTIC ARTISAN EXPERIENCE COMBINING TEA, ART AND NATURE.

We take pride in cultivating our home-grown leaves, blending the finest organic teas and ingredients from carefully sourced farms, and hand-crafting ceramics to accompany the flavours. This holistic craft invites a deeper connection with ourselves and with nature. Tea has a unique power to heal, transform and soothe. Our teas enhance these experiences by creating space to breathe and find balance in busyness. Whether you are creating that space for peace in solitude or community, tea is a celebration of truly being connected.

TEA REPRESENTS HOW WE ARE CONNECTED TO THE LANDSCAPE AND TO EACH OTHER.

Our approach to roasting is governed by two overarching principles: flavour and freshness.

Honestea has been roasting coffee in Seoul, Korea for four generations. We believe people have intrinsic value and seek to honour our coffee producers, our staff, and our customers by only serving the freshest, best tasting coffee possible. To this end, we source the highest quality green coffee and roast it in small batches daily, guided by one principle: the best taste wins.
